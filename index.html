WELCOME TO MORAZHA CO-OPERATIVE ARTS & SCIENCE COLLEGE

Morazha Co-operative Arts & Science College is Nestled on the Muthuvani Hill, in Morazha Village, a beautiful landscape, by the side of Ozhakrome- Vellikkeel Road, just 3 km away from the Kannur University, Manghattuparamba Campus, sixteen kilometers north of Kannur Town and six kilometers west of Thaliparamba town, 5 km east of Kannapuram Railway Station. It is an ideal location which is conducive for academic, intellectual and physical pursuits. The students are very fortunate to have their college at a sight with its beauty and soothing atmosphere. The College started functioning with 8 Degree Programmes (BA English, BCA, BBM, BSc Computer Science and B.Com (Cooperation) in August 2008 and is the realization of a long cherished dream of the enterprising people of Morazha. The permanent building of the College was inaugurated by Sri. G. Sudhakaran, Hon. Minister for Co-operation in the presence of Vice - Chancellor of the Kannur University on 21st January 2010. We are fortunate enough to get B.Com Computer Application, and 1 PG Courses (M.Com Finance and MSc Computer Science) were started in 2011. The College has at present 8 Degree Courses and 1 PG Courses.
